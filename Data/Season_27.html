The twenty-seventh season of the animated television series The Simpsons will premiere on Fox in the United States on September 27, 2015. [2] On October 28, 2014, executive producer Al Jean announced that Season 27 went into production, [3] renewing the series through the 2015–16 season. [4]

Some guest stars for this season include Kristen Bell, David Copperfield, Lena Dunham and Kelsey Grammer. Carl Zealer, who won a competition to be animated into The Simpsons, will be in this season. [5]

On May 14, 2015, it was announced that Harry Shearer had quit voicing characters for The Simpsons, such as Ned Flanders, Mr. Burns, Seymour Skinner, Otto, Waylon Smithers, Lenny, Rainier Wolfcastle, Reverend Lovejoy, Jasper, Dr. Hibbert, Kent Brockman, Dewey Largo, Scratchy, and many others. The characters will be re-cast but it is currently unknown of who is replacing Shearer.

Confirmed plots and guest stars [edit]

  • In "'Cue Detective", Homer will delve into the underground world of barbecues. [21] It is written by Joel H. Cohen and directed by Timothy Bailey. [22] [23] [6]
  • Kelsey Grammer will reprise his role of Sideshow Bob in "Treehouse of Horror XXVI" [24] written by Joel H. Cohen and directed by Steven Dean Moore. [25] [6] The episode will include a segment where Bob finally kills Bart. [26] However, as Treehouse of Horror episodes are non-canon, Bart's demise will not be permanent and he will appear alive as normal in the next episode.
  • In "Friend with Benefit", Kristen Bell will guest star as Lisa's rich friend Harper, whose father becomes friends with Homer, but Lisa becomes concerned that Harper is a little too entitled. Also, David Copperfield will guest star in this episode. [27] It is written by Rob LaZebnik and directed by Matthew Faughnan. [28] [6]
  • "Paths of Glory" is written by Michael Ferris and directed by Steven Dean Moore [29]
  • "Gal of Constant Sorrow" is written by Carolyn Omine. [30]
  • "Puffless" is written by J. Stewart Burns and directed by Rob Oliver. [31] [32]
  • "Lisa with an 'S'" is written by Stephanie Gillis and directed by Bob Anderson. [33] [6]
  • An upcoming episode, written by Michael Price, will involve Apu. [34]
  • "Barthood" will be a flashback/forward episode which is a parody of 2014 film Boyhood . [35] It is written by Dan Greaney and directed by Rob Oliver. [36] [37]
  • "Halloween of Horror" is a Halloween-themed episode, but unlike the "Treehouse of Horror" episodes, it will be part of the series' canon. [38] [39] The episode is directed by Mike B. Anderson and written by Carolyn Omine. [40] [41]
  • "Love Is in the N2-O2-Ar-CO2-Ne-He-CH4" will be a Valentine's Day themed episode, and it will air on February 14, 2016. [19] It will involve Professor Frink and it is written by John Frink. [42]
  • Spider-Pig from The Simpsons Movie will return. [26]
